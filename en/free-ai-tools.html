Free AI tools

Build a free AI tools workflow for beginner content creation.

Many free AI tools have free access or free plans, but limits and included features can change. Always check each official website before relying on a tool for client work or a serious project.

ChatGPT

Useful for ideas, outlines, summaries and editing. Free access exists, but model availability and limits can change by plan and region.

Canva

Good for thumbnails, carousels and simple PDFs. The free plan is enough for many early tests, while some assets and AI features may require paid access.

CapCut

Helpful for short-video editing, captions, pacing and formatting. Some advanced effects or export options may move between free and paid tiers.

Google Trends

Useful for comparing search interest and spotting seasonal topics. Treat it as a signal, not a complete market research tool.

Google Docs and Sheets

Use Docs for drafts and Sheets for content calendars, status tracking and prompt libraries.

Notion

Good for organizing notes, templates and content plans. Free plans are useful for individuals, while team and AI features may vary.

Starter workflow

Research with Google Trends, draft with ChatGPT, design in Canva, edit short clips in CapCut, and track the calendar in Sheets or Notion.
