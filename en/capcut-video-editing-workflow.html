CapCut workflow

Use CapCut as an editing tool, not as the business model.

CapCut can help with captions, cuts, pacing, formatting and short-video exports. The value still comes from the idea, script, clarity and publishing consistency.

Captions

Pacing

Formatting

Prepare vertical exports for Shorts, TikTok and Reels with safe margins.

Review

Watch the final video without sound and then with sound before publishing.
