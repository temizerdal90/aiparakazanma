TikTok prompts

Plan TikTok videos with AI content ideas, hooks and captions.

These TikTok video prompts help you turn a useful idea into a short, focused video plan while keeping the tone natural.

Hook prompt

Write 20 first-three-second hooks for a TikTok about [topic]. Make them specific and avoid unrealistic claims.

Caption prompt

Write 10 TikTok captions for [video idea]. Include one clear takeaway and a gentle question for comments.

Hashtag prompt

Suggest 12 TikTok hashtags for [niche]: 4 broad, 4 niche and 4 beginner-friendly. Pick the best 5.

CapCut editing plan

Create a CapCut editing plan for this script: [script]. Include cuts, captions, b-roll ideas and pacing notes.

7-day TikTok plan

  1. Day 1Answer one beginner question.
  2. Day 2Show a simple workflow in three steps.
  3. Day 3Share a mistake to avoid.
  4. Day 4Compare two tools with a fair use case.
  5. Day 5Turn a comment into a video idea.
  6. Day 6Create a checklist video.
  7. Day 7Review saves, comments and completion rate.
