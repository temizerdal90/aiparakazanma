AI safety and ethics

Follow AI content safety basics for ethical AI content and copyright review.

Responsible AI content is reviewed, original enough to be useful and respectful of rights, policies and audience trust.

Copyright

Use licensed media, original writing and clear attribution when needed. Do not copy scripts or visuals from other creators.

Misinformation

Fact-check claims, dates, product details, health topics, legal topics and financial statements.

Platform rules

Review the rules of YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and ad networks before scaling a workflow.

Spam

Avoid mass posting, keyword stuffing, bot traffic, copied comments and misleading calls to action.

Reused content

AI narration over copied clips is not a strong content strategy. Add original structure, insight and editing.

Human review

Read, watch and verify the final result before publishing or delivering it to anyone.

Safety review prompt

Review this content plan for copyright risk, misleading claims, spam signals, reused content, ad policy issues and missing human review steps: [plan]
