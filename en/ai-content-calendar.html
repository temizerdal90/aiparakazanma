AI content calendar

Build an AI content calendar for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and blog ideas.

An AI content calendar is not just a list of dates. It connects audience questions, formats, production steps and review metrics.

7-day plan

  1. Day 1Pick one audience question.
  2. Day 2Create a short video script.
  3. Day 3Turn the script into a carousel outline.
  4. Day 4Write a blog or newsletter outline.
  5. Day 5Design a thumbnail or cover.
  6. Day 6Publish and note the result.
  7. Day 7Repurpose the strongest idea.

30-day prompt

Create a 30-day content calendar for [audience] about [topic]. Include platform, format, hook, key point, CTA and review metric.

Platform examples

Turn this topic into one YouTube Short, one TikTok, one Instagram Reel and one blog outline: [topic].

Review prompt

Here are my results: [metrics]. Suggest what I should repeat, improve and stop next week.
