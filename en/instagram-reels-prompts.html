30-day Reels plan
Create a 30-day Instagram Reels content plan for [topic].
For each day, include:
1. Video title
2. First 3-second hook
3. 30-second script
4. On-screen text
5. Canva cover idea
6. CapCut editing idea
7. Caption and hashtags.
Multi-page post
Create a 7-page Instagram multi-page post about [topic].
For each page, include:
- Short headline
- One-sentence explanation
- Visual idea
- Design note
End with a call to visit the related guide.
