Instagram Reels prompts

Use these prompts with ChatGPT or Gemini, then turn the result into visuals and videos with Canva and CapCut.

Prompt: 30-day Reels plan

Create a 30-day Instagram Reels content plan for [topic].
For each day, include:
1. Video title
2. First 3-second hook
3. 30-second script
4. On-screen text
5. Canva cover idea
6. CapCut editing idea
7. Caption and hashtags.

Workflow

ChatGPT → Canva → CapCut → Instagram Reels.
