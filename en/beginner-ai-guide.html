AI for beginners

Start with one useful AI workflow, not every new tool.

AI can help you research, outline, draft, edit, plan and repurpose content. It is most useful when you give it a clear goal and review the output carefully.

What AI helps with

Research, drafts and structure

Use AI to organize ideas, compare angles, write first drafts, create outlines and turn one topic into several content formats.

Where to start

Pick a format

Choose blog, short video, social media or digital products. Then build a simple weekly workflow around that one format.

Useful tools

Keep the stack small

Start with ChatGPT, Canva, CapCut, Google Trends and a simple tracker in Sheets or Notion.

Common mistakes

Avoid raw AI output

Do not publish unreviewed content, copied claims, vague advice or content that ignores platform rules.

First 7-day plan

  1. Day 1Choose one audience and one content format.
  2. Day 2List 20 beginner questions with ChatGPT and Google Trends.
  3. Day 3Create three outlines or short scripts.
  4. Day 4Design one visual or edit one short clip.
  5. Day 5Publish one useful piece of content.
  6. Day 6Repurpose it into another format.
  7. Day 7Review saves, comments, clicks and questions.
