YouTube Shorts prompts

Create AI YouTube Shorts ideas with hooks, scripts and titles.

Use these YouTube Shorts script prompts to plan educational short videos that are specific, useful and safe from exaggerated claims.

Hook prompt

Write 15 YouTube Shorts hooks about [topic] for [audience]. Keep them clear, curious and realistic. Avoid income promises.

Script prompt

Create a 30-second Shorts script about [topic]. Include 0-3s hook, three short teaching points, screen text and a soft CTA.

Title prompt

Suggest 20 YouTube Shorts titles for [topic]. Make them natural, beginner-friendly and not clickbait.

Thumbnail ideas

Give me 10 simple thumbnail ideas for a Shorts video about [topic]. Include text overlay ideas with 2-4 words.

7-day Shorts plan

  1. Day 1Explain one beginner mistake.
  2. Day 2Show a simple before-and-after workflow.
  3. Day 3Answer one common question.
  4. Day 4Share a tool comparison without declaring a winner for everyone.
  5. Day 5Turn a blog idea into a 30-second script.
  6. Day 6Publish a checklist-style Short.
  7. Day 7Review retention, comments and saved ideas.
