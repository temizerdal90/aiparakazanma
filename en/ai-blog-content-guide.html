AI blog content

Write blog posts with AI using outlines, SEO checks and human editing.

AI can speed up research and drafting, but useful AI blog content still needs clear search intent, examples, fact-checking and internal links.

Topic research

Use Google Trends, search suggestions and audience questions to find useful topics.

Outline prompts

Ask for H2/H3 structure, reader intent, examples and missing subtopics before drafting.

Drafting with ChatGPT

Draft one section at a time and keep your own examples, screenshots or notes in the article.

Fact-checking

Verify dates, product features, legal claims and platform rules before publishing.

SEO without stuffing

Use natural headings, answer the topic clearly and avoid repeating keywords mechanically.

Internal linking

Link to related guides such as prompt library and content calendar.

Blog outline prompt

Create a blog outline for [topic] for beginners. Include search intent, H2/H3 headings, examples, internal link ideas and claims that need fact-checking.
