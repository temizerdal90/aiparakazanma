AI prompt library

Use copy paste AI prompts for practical content creation workflows.

ChatGPT prompts work best when you add context: audience, topic, format, tone, constraints and what you want to avoid.

Video prompt

Create a short video script about [topic] for [audience]. Include hook, three teaching points, screen text, b-roll ideas and a soft CTA. Avoid exaggerated income claims.

Blog prompt

Write a blog outline about [topic] for beginners. Include H2/H3 headings, search intent, examples, common mistakes and a balanced conclusion.

Social media prompt

Turn [idea] into 5 social posts: one Reel, one carousel, one short caption, one question post and one checklist post.

Product idea prompt

Suggest 10 simple digital product ideas for [audience] who struggle with [problem]. Include format, use case and validation question.

Content calendar prompt

Create a 14-day content calendar for [platform] about [niche]. Include topic, format, hook, CTA and review metric.

Editing prompt

Review this draft for clarity, accuracy, tone and unsupported claims. Suggest a cleaner version for beginners: [draft]

How to use prompts well

Do not publish raw AI output without reviewing it. Add examples, remove claims you cannot support and keep the final content useful for a real person.
