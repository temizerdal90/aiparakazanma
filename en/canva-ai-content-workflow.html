Canva workflow

Use a Canva content workflow to turn AI ideas into social media templates.

Canva is useful for social posts, carousel slides, thumbnails, template packs and simple PDFs. The goal is consistency, not decoration.

Social posts

Turn one idea into a square post, story slide and short caption graphic.

Carousel posts

Use a clear title slide, one idea per slide and a final summary or CTA.

Thumbnail ideas

Keep text short, contrast strong and the topic obvious on mobile screens.

Templates

Make repeatable layouts for quotes, checklists, comparisons and tutorials.

Design consistency

Limit fonts, colors and spacing choices so each post feels like part of one system.

Canva prompt

Create a Canva design brief for [content idea]. Include format, layout, text hierarchy, image style, color direction and export notes.

Carousel prompt

Turn [topic] into a 7-slide carousel with title, slide headlines, short body text and a final practical takeaway.
