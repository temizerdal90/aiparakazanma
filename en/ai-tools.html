AI tools for content creation

Choose AI tools for beginners by the workflow they support.

A beginner AI tool stack should stay simple: research, draft, design, edit, publish and review. Add more tools only when they remove a real bottleneck.

ChatGPT

Useful for ideas, outlines, scripts, summaries, editing and content repurposing. Always review facts and claims.

Canva

Helpful for social graphics, carousels, thumbnails, PDFs and simple digital product layouts.

CapCut

Good for short video editing, captions, pacing and turning a script into publishable clips.

ElevenLabs

Can support voiceover workflows where licensing, disclosure and platform rules are respected.

Google Trends

Use it to compare interest, spot seasonal topics and avoid guessing blindly.

Notion or Google Sheets

Track ideas, status, publishing dates, results and reusable prompts.

Simple stack for beginners

Start with ChatGPT for planning, Canva for visuals, CapCut for short video edits and a spreadsheet for tracking. A simple workflow you use every week is better than a crowded tool list.
