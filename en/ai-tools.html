Which AI tool should you use?

Do not start by subscribing to every tool. Start with the task: text, design, video, voice, image or research.

Text and ideas

Use ChatGPT, Claude or Gemini for ideas, scripts, captions, outlines and prompts.

ChatGPTClaudeGemini

Design

Use Canva for posts, covers, thumbnails and carousel-style visuals.

Short video

Use CapCut to edit Reels, Shorts and TikTok videos with captions and fast cuts.

CapCutShortsTikTok

Voiceover

Use ElevenLabs for faceless videos, narration and AI voice workflows.

ElevenLabsVoice

AI video

Use Runway, Pika or Kling for generated scenes, then edit in CapCut.
