Tool selection

Choose AI tools by your workflow, not by the loudest feature list.

The right tool depends on what you want to create, how much time you have and how comfortable you are with editing.

Video creators

ChatGPT + Canva + CapCut

Plan scripts with ChatGPT, create visuals in Canva and edit short clips in CapCut.

Bloggers

ChatGPT + Google Trends + Docs

Research topics, outline articles, draft carefully and fact-check before publishing.

Social creators

ChatGPT + Canva + calendar

Create captions, carousel outlines, Reels ideas and a repeatable posting plan.

Digital products

ChatGPT + Canva + Sheets

Plan templates, checklists, prompt packs and simple planners, then validate demand.

No budget

Use fewer tools

Start with free access where available and avoid subscriptions until you know your workflow.
