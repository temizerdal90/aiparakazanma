ChatGPT workflows

Make money with ChatGPT by building useful content workflows.

ChatGPT prompts can help you move from blank page to structured draft for scripts, blog outlines, product ideas and social content. The real value comes from your niche choice, editing, examples and consistency.

Useful workflows

Start with repeatable tasks that improve your output.

Content ideas

Ask for topic angles, audience pain points and beginner questions, then choose the ideas you can explain with real examples.

Scripts and outlines

Turn one topic into a short video script, blog outline, carousel structure and email draft.

Digital product ideas

Brainstorm checklists, templates, swipe files and planning sheets around a specific audience need.

Editing and clarity

Use ChatGPT to simplify, shorten, rewrite and organize, but review every claim before publishing.

10 copy-paste prompts

  • Give me 20 beginner-friendly content ideas about [topic] for [audience]. Avoid hype and income promises.
  • Create a 30-second short video script about [topic] with hook, three points and a soft call to action.
  • Turn this idea into a blog outline with H2 and H3 headings: [idea].
  • Suggest 10 digital product ideas for [audience] that solve small, practical problems.
  • Rewrite this draft in clear English for beginners: [draft].
  • Create five title options for [topic] that sound natural and not clickbait.
  • Build a 7-day content plan for [platform] around [niche].
  • List the risks, assumptions and missing details in this idea: [idea].
  • Create a simple offer description for a content planning service for [business type].
  • Repurpose this blog idea into a video script, carousel and email: [idea].
